To show the breadth and detail of the library, here's a preview of the complete Javadoc listing of interfaces. Readers of the Schema specification may note that every schema component is modeled. There are also additional convenience interfaces, such as XSDDiagnostic (which reports problems in a schema) and XSDSimpleTypeDefinition.Assessment (which reports an assessment of a Node versus the type).
